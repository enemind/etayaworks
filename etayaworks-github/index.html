Who We Are

We Build the Dreams of a Better Future

Etayaworks Building Company was founded on a single conviction: that every client deserves to see their vision rise from a blueprint into a living, breathing structure. We combine rigorous engineering with creative craftsmanship to deliver buildings that stand the test of time.

Based in Nairobi, we work across Kenya — from private residences to large-scale commercial developments. Our multidisciplinary team handles everything from the first sketch to the final key handover.

How We Work

Your Project, Step by Step

1

Initial Consultation

We meet to understand your vision, goals, site, and budget. No jargon — just a frank conversation about what's possible.

2

Design & Planning

Our architects and engineers produce detailed drawings, BOQs, and a realistic project timeline for your approval.

3

Approvals & Permits

We handle all county approvals, NCA registration, and NEMA requirements — so you don't have to chase bureaucracy.

4

Construction Phase

Our crews break ground. You receive weekly photo reports, site visits are always welcome, and our PM is reachable 24/7.

5

Quality Inspection

Before handover, every system — structural, electrical, plumbing, finishing — is inspected against specification.

6

Handover & Aftercare

We hand over your keys with full documentation, warranties, and a 12-month defects liability period. We don't disappear after completion.
